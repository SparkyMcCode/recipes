Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 3/4 cup cocoa
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup canola oil
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 1 cup boiling water
 - 2 tsps matcha 

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.

 2. In a small bowl, matcha and combine 2 oz. boiling water. Wisk until matcha is completely dissolved there should be a thin foam on top.
 
 3. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in matcha water and boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool completely.
